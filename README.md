## Features

* **Auto-detect Roblox cookie:** Seamlessly logs you in by detecting your Roblox session cookie.
* **Instant animation upload:** Upload animations directly from the app without hassle.
* **Instant audio upload:** Upload audio files directly from the app and spoof them in your games.
* **Link to Roblox Studio:** Quickly connect your uploaded animations and audio to the [ISpooferMotion plugin for Roblox Studio](https://create.roblox.com/store/asset/77166107193979/ISpooferMotion) for real-time spoofing.

## How It Works

1. **Login:** The app automatically detects your Roblox cookie or you can enter it manually.
2. **Upload assets:** Upload animations **and audio files** instantly to Roblox.
3. **Apply spoofing:** Use the official ISpooferMotion Roblox Studio plugin (linked above) to link and spoof animations and audio in your game projects.
4. **Spoof in-game:**

   * **Animations:** Replaces animation objects in scripts and game assets.
   * **Audio:** Replaces audio assets in scripts or directly in game objects, allowing you to override sounds seamlessly.

## Usage

1. Install and launch the ISpooferMotion app.
2. Install the Studio plugin: [ISpooferMotion plugin for Roblox Studio](https://create.roblox.com/store/asset/77166107193979/ISpooferMotion) or get it from the releases tab.
3. In Roblox Studio, go to the Plugins tab and find the ISpooferMotion plugin.
4. Click the **Get IDs** button to open a window showing your asset IDs.
5. Copy the animation and audio IDs from the plugin window.
6. Paste them into the ISpooferMotion app.
7. Enter your Roblox cookie manually or use **Auto Detect Cookie**.
8. Optionally, enter a **Group ID** to upload assets to a group you manage.
9. Click **Run Spoofer** and wait for the output.
10. Copy the output and go back to Studio. Click **Replace IDs** in the plugin tab.
11. Paste the output into the textbox and click **Run**.
12. Your animations and audio files are now spoofed and replaced in your game!
